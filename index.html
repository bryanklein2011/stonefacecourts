About Us

Welcome to Stoneface Courts, Albuquerque’s premier 10-court sand volleyball facility. Our courts are open for leagues, tournaments, and special events for all ages and skill levels.

We run exciting doubles, 4's, and 6's tournaments all year long, bringing competitive fun to every level of play. One of our proudest highlights is the Sandia Classic Doubles Tournament — one of the biggest beach-only volleyball tournaments in the country.

Beyond tournaments, we offer adult and youth training programs to help players grow their skills and confidence. Whether you're just starting or leveling up, our coaches and community will support you every step of the way.

Looking for a unique venue? We offer rentals for birthday parties, corporate events, and more. We also host charity tournaments and fundraisers, bringing people together for great causes through the game we love.

Leagues

We offer leagues Tuesday through Friday from 6 PM to 10 PM, with early and late games available. Whether you're in it for fun or fierce competition, we’ve got a league for you.

Our league calendar follows the seasons — Winter, Spring, Summer, and Fall — giving you a chance to play all year long! Each season brings a fresh start, new teams, and a whole lot of excitement.

From beginners to seasoned players, our league nights are filled with laughter, energy, and competitive spirit. Join one or all four and see why our community keeps coming back season after season!
